History

The Lesotho Cultural and Heritage Festival began in 2018 to promote Basotho culture and tourism. The festival has grown into an important tourism attraction that showcases traditional dances, music, clothing and crafts.

Significance

The festival helps preserve Basotho traditions and encourages international tourists to learn about Lesotho’s culture while supporting local businesses and artists.

Target Tourists

  • Local tourists
  • International tourists
  • Travel bloggers
  • Cultural researchers
  • Families and students
