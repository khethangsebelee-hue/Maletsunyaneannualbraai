Event Overview

The Lesotho Cultural and Heritage Festival is a tourism event designed to celebrate the traditions, music, food and lifestyle of the Basotho people. The festival attracts local and international tourists who want to experience the beauty of Lesotho's culture and heritage.

Event Date

10 – 12 December 2026

Location

Maseru, Lesotho – Setsoto Stadium Cultural Grounds
